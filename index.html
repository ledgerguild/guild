FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


How to join LEDGER?

We have our own recruitment process at Discord. Please see the #recruitment-guide channel for more details.

What are the requirements to join?

At least 10 million PVE fame, can adapt to ZvZ nature of the game, and Discord (COMMUNICATION IS THE BEST THE WEAPON IN THE WAR ZONE).

I don't know what builds / gears to use. What now?

Since we're a guild that focuses on ZvZ's, we make sure that the members are well-equipped. With that said, we'll be there to help.

I cannot always join the Call-To-Arms (CTA) for the ZvZ. Is that okay?

One of your major obligations as a ledger member is to attend our mandatory CTA's. When we say "mandatory", we mean come join if you are online. you don't need to come online just for our mandatory CTAs, because real life comes first.

If you are online and you can't join because of something, as long as it's valid, whisper an officer for a permission to skip the CTA.

I am a gatherer / crafter. Can I still join LEDGER?

Yes! You are more than welcome to join. But if you're open to the possibility of joining ZvZ's, that would be nice!

Do you have Gathering Tax?

Unlike most established guilds, we don't tax our gatherers based on their gathering fame per week. Our only "gathering tax" are aspects. When somebody spots an aspect, he/she is required to report that for members to help in killing and gathering the aspect.

Half of the gathered materials belongs to the guild, and the other half belongs to the party responsible for the aspect.
